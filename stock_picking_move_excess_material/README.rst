==================================
Stock picking move excess material
==================================

* In internal picking, new button "move excess material". By pressing the
  button, bring to the delivery note lines all the products whose actual
  stock - reserved stock in the origin location of the delivery note type
  is greater than zero. The difference will be put in the amount of
  the internal picking line.

========================
Stock Quant Expiry Dates
========================

New two related fields in quants, mrp date and expiry date defined in the lot.
Lifespan progress in quants. Lifespan is the difference in days between mrp
date and the expiry date of the lot defined in the quant. The progress shows
the percentage of the lifespan reached in time.

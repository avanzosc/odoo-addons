========================
Stock Move Sale Quantity
========================

* Unlink not delivered sale lines with Consumible or Product type products when SO is in state Sale Order.
* Cancel stock moves to be unlinked when sale lines unlinked.
* Update stock move quantity on lower sale line quantity.
